# Vertical Video Export

Status: Draft
Last updated: 2026-07-16

## Goal

Export a board as a simple vertical video with synchronized photos and rendered audio.

## Direction

Use 9:16 output, one image per musical section, simple cuts or transitions, local save or system sharing.

## Non-Goals

- Templates, animated text, waveform visuals, manual timing, extra filters, direct publishing.

## Open Questions

- Offline audio rendering architecture, export performance, and Photos permissions.
